About Ghosted

Ghosted is the clan system around the vghosted live channel.

Twitch gives Ghosted its spark, Discord gives it durability, and the App Hub gives it utility. This page explains how those layers work together without collapsing into a generic community site.

Watch Twitch Join Discord

How It Works

Ghosted stays simple on purpose.

Community products get muddy when every surface tries to do everything. Ghosted works because each layer has one job.

01 / Discover

Twitch creates the first pull.

People discover Ghosted through live content, shared nights, and stream energy around the vghosted channel.

02 / Belong

Discord becomes the social operating layer.

Membership, roles, voice, coordination, and continuity all sit in the Discord layer where the community already lives.

03 / Act

The app gives members useful behavior.

Rewards, giveaways, profile sync, community data, and casino play create repeat visits beyond the stream itself.

Activity Board

What Changed

The UI now leaves room for real art, real data, and real member behavior.

Instead of hard-coding every page into the same purple card language, the new system creates composition slots. That means Ghosted can grow sharper over time without rebuilding layout foundations again.

Design principles in the new system

One dominant idea per section. Fewer surface levels. Stronger typography. Calmer operational pages. Theatrical emphasis only where it adds meaning, especially on the casino floor.
