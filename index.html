About the Clan

Ghosted is a social OSRS clan with a live pulse and clear ways to plug in.

Some people show up for the stream, some for the events, and some because they want a regular place to hang out. The goal is the same: make it easy to find your people and something to do.

01 / Clan energy

Laid-back, but not lifeless.

Ghosted is built for regulars, returning players, grinders, and lurkers who want a clan that stays warm without feeling stiff.

02 / Shared activities

There is usually something to rally around.

Bossing, skilling, competitions, giveaways, and stream nights give members easy ways to join in without needing a hard reset every visit.

03 / Member loop

The systems support the social side.

The app does not replace the clan. It keeps rewards, profiles, giveaways, and progress organized so the community can stay the main event.

What Members Do

The clan runs on conversation, events, and a few shared systems that keep momentum up.

Discord is where people gather. Twitch adds the live spark. The app handles the practical side so members can check balances, track profiles, enter giveaways, and stay connected without hunting through chat.
